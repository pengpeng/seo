## Slide copy

### Title

**The Personal Cloud is A Future Trillion-Dollar Market**

We are on the verge of an explosive increase in demand for compute power.

### Three enablers

1. **Competitive local LLMs** — Open-weight models now match last year's SOTA on consumer hardware.
2. **Production-ready open-source apps** — A mature ecosystem of alternatives to Google, Microsoft, and OpenAI services.
3. **Enterprise-grade cloud-native infrastructure** — Kubernetes, service mesh, and GPU scheduling proven at datacenter scale.

### Market math

- **100×** — Projected growth in individual compute requirements.
- **$10T** — Global public cloud revenue annually by 2035.
- **10%** — Share of users that will choose personal cloud:
  - Privacy & compliance: full ownership of personal data.
  - Performance: low-latency AI inference without cloud round-trips.
  - Cost: avoid per-call cloud bills as usage scales.

### The 2026 takeoff

By late 2026, the best AI tools from 2025 will run locally on consumer hardware.

**AI is getting smaller and better:**

- 2022 SOTA GPT-3.5 → 2023 Consumer Llama2 13B
- 2023 SOTA GPT-4 → 2024 Consumer Gemma-2 27B
- 2024 SOTA GPT-o1 → 2025 Consumer Qwen3-30B-A3B
- 2025 SOTA Claude Opus 4.5 → 2026 Consumer Qwen3.6-27B
