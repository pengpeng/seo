## Slide copy

### Title

**What is Olares?**

An open-source Personal Cloud OS you operate in plain language, built to run local AI agents and LLMs on hardware you own.

### A Personal Agent for everyone

AI is shifting from apps to agents. In the near future, everyone will have a Personal Agent: an always-available assistant that handles work and daily tasks, manages personal information, and runs their digital life, reachable from any device.

### A Personal Cloud to run it

A single, always-on, always-accessible interface and a unified execution environment for a person and all of their AI devices.

- Windows → runs the Personal Computer
- Android → runs the Smartphone
- Olares → runs the Personal Cloud

### Who is Olares for?

- Prosumers, small businesses, and privacy-conscious users who want self-hosted AI agents.
- Users motivated by privacy, data ownership, controllability, and long-term cost.

### What problem does Olares solve?

- Public-cloud agents force you to surrender context, runtime, and actions to third parties.
- Olares keeps agents and AI on your own hardware: NAS, box, PC, Raspberry Pi, or a cluster composed of multiple computing devices.
- Operate in plain language without managing servers, containers, networks, storage, or AI infrastructure.
