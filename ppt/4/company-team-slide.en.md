### Company facts

- Project initiated in April, 2022, ahead of the generative-AI wave.
- Headquartered in Singapore, with offices in Beijing, Japan, and North America.
- $100M+ raised to date, with a Series A round led by SIG (Susquehanna International Group), and a $50M round closed in April, 2026, backed by top-tier global investors and founders who shaped the consumer internet era.

### Team

### Olares One: the "G1" for personal cloud

In 2008, Google launched the G1, G2, and G3 prototypes with HTC before other manufacturers began producing Android phones.

We believe Olares is currently in its **"Android 1.0" stage.**

By launching Olares One, we aim to promote the Olares system, validate the personal cloud market, and attract more developers to the ecosystem.
